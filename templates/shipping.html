Shipping Policy

This website operates an Automated Vending Machine system that provides instant, on-site delivery of products.

No physical shipping or courier delivery is involved. All products are dispensed immediately from the vending machine after successful payment.

Once a customer completes a payment using the QR code displayed on the vending machine, the machine automatically verifies the payment and dispenses the selected item.

Since this is an automated, location-based service:

  • No shipping addresses are collected
  • No delivery timelines apply
  • No third-party logistics or courier services are used

In the event of a technical failure where payment is successful but the item is not dispensed, the transaction will be handled according to our Cancellations and Refunds Policy.

This shipping policy applies only to products dispensed via the Automated Vending Machine and does not involve any form of physical shipping.

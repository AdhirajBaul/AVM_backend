Automated Vending Machine

How Payments Work

Payments are made by scanning a UPI QR code displayed on the vending machine. The QR code redirects users to a secure Razorpay-hosted payment page.

Once payment is successful, the vending machine automatically dispenses the item.

Refunds & Support

If a payment is deducted but the item is not dispensed, the amount will be refunded automatically or within 5–7 working days.
